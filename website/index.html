About Orphosys

Empowering the Future Through Cognitive Growth

Orphosys Foundation is one of the best Kolkata based Child Brain Development Institution, dealing with several scientifically proven technologies of identifying child inner capabilities and polishing them to reach their heights.

We are the fastest growing brand with multiple training programs like DMIT, Abacus, Vedic Math, Robotics and many more. Our vision is to reach out to every household and contribute to the nation by developing young geniuses.
